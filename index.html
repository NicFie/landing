How it works

It transfers nutrients

Water, with its amazing dissolving properties, is the perfect medium for transmitting substances, such as phosphates or calcium ions, into and out of a cell.

Important in chemical reactions

It enables key chemical reactions in animal, plant and microbial cells.

Essential building block

The polarity of water also helps the formation of the delicate membranes that encapsulate all living cells.
